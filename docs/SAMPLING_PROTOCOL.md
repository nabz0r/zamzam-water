# Zamzam Water Sampling Protocol

## 1. Obtaining Zamzam Water

### Official Sources
- **King Abdullah Zamzam Water Distribution Center** (Kudai, Mecca)
  - Official 5L/10L sealed containers
  - Certificate of authenticity from Saudi Geological Survey (SGS)
  - Available to Hajj/Umrah pilgrims
- **Direct collection at Masjid Al-Haram**
  - Zamzam taps around the Mataf area
  - Use sterile sampling containers (pre-rinsed with ultrapure water)

### Sample Documentation
- Record exact time, date, and GPS coordinates of collection
- Note tap ID or container batch number
- Photograph the collection point and certificate
- Chain of custody form signed by collector

## 2. Transport Conditions

| Parameter | Requirement |
|-----------|------------|
| Container | HDPE or PTFE (acid-washed, trace-metal grade) |
| Volume | Minimum 2L per analysis set |
| Temperature | 4°C (cooler with ice packs, NOT frozen) |
| Light | Opaque container or wrapped in aluminum foil |
| Headspace | Minimal (fill to top for dissolved gas preservation) |
| Acidification | 1 aliquot acidified with HNO₃ to pH<2 (for metals) |
| Timeline | Lab delivery within 72h of collection |
| Duplicates | Minimum 2 replicates per collection point |

### Shipping
- Use insulated cooler with temperature logger
- International shipping: declare as "water sample for scientific analysis"
- Customs: attach sampling certificate and research institution letter

## 4. Analyses to Request

### Tier 1 — Core Chemistry (~€500-800)
- **ICP-MS full scan** (52+ elements including REE)
  - Ca, Mg, Na, K, Li, Sr, Ba, Fe, Mn, Zn, Cu
  - As, Pb, Cd, Cr, Ni, Se, Sb (trace metals)
  - Rare earth elements (La, Ce, Nd — provenance tracers)
- **Ion chromatography**: F⁻, Cl⁻, Br⁻, NO₃⁻, SO₄²⁻, PO₄³⁻
- **Alkalinity**: HCO₃⁻, CO₃²⁻ (titration)
- **Physical**: pH, EC, TDS, turbidity, temperature

### Tier 2 — Isotope Hydrology (~€800-1500)
- **δ¹⁸O and δ²H** (IRMS): origin and evaporation history
- **Tritium (³H)**: modern recharge indicator (<70 years)
- **¹⁴C of DIC**: radiometric age (100–40,000 years)
- **δ³⁴S and δ¹⁸O-SO₄**: sulfate origin tracing
- **⁸⁷Sr/⁸⁶Sr ratio**: aquifer rock interaction

### Tier 3 — Microbiology (~€600-1000)
- **16S rRNA amplicon sequencing**: bacterial community profiling
- **Shotgun metagenomics**: full microbiome characterization
- **Culture-based**: total coliforms, E. coli, HPC
- **qPCR**: specific pathogen detection

### Tier 4 — Advanced (~€1000-2000)
- **Noble gases** (He, Ne, Ar, Kr, Xe): recharge temperature
- **Dissolved organic carbon (DOC)** + fluorescence EEM
- **Pharmaceuticals screening** (LC-MS/MS): anthropogenic contamination

## 6. Quality Assurance

- Request ISO 17025 accredited methods
- Include field blank (ultrapure water through same containers)
- Include certified reference material (e.g., NIST SRM 1640a)
- Request full uncertainty budget with results
- All results as digital CSV + official PDF certificate
