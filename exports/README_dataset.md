# Zamzam Water Chemical Composition Dataset

## Overview

- **Total measurements**: 177
- **Sources**: 16 (5 Zamzam studies + 11 comparison waters)
- **Elements**: 25 distinct parameters
- **Temporal coverage**: 2009–2025

## Zamzam studies

- Bhardwaj 2023 (BJSTR) — ICP-MS / ICP-OES (DOI: 10.26717/BJSTR.2023.48.007677)
- Donia & Mortada 2021 (Heliyon) — ICP-OES / Ion Chromatography (DOI: 10.1016/j.heliyon.2021.e07346)
- Shomar 2012 (J Water Health / Springer) — ICP-MS / IC (DOI: 10.2166/wh.2012.081)
- Al-Gamal 2009 (Environ Geol) — Standard methods / Mass spectrometry (DOI: 10.1007/s00254-009-1352-8)
- EJEBA 2025 Review (aggregated) — Literature review aggregate
